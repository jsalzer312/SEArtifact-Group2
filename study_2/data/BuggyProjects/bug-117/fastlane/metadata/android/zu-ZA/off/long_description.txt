Skena imikhiqizo yokudla noma ibhakhodi yabo ukuthola idatha kumazitha, izithasiselo namaqiniso okudla.

Uhlelo lokusebenza luvumela ukubuka imikhiqizo engu-547.000 (kufaka phakathi i-175.000 e-US) esivele iqukethe i-database evulekile nevulekile ye-Open Food Facts, futhi futhi ukwengeza izithombe nedatha yemikhiqizo elahlekile.

Ukuze uthole iphrojekthi ye-Open Food Facts, sicela uvakashele ku-https: //openfoodfacts.org


*** Nika isandla ekudleni kokuphawula ***

- I-Open Food Facts iyinhlangano yemininingwane yokudla eyenziwe yilowo nalowo, wonke umuntu.
- Ungayisebenzisa ukwenza ukukhetha okungcono kokudla, futhi njengoba kuvuliwe idatha, noma ubani angayisebenzisa kabusha nganoma iyiphi injongo.
- I-Open Food Facts yinqubo engeyona inzuzo eyenziwe yizinkulungwane zamavolontiya avela emhlabeni jikelele. Sinezinhlelo eziningi ezithakazelisayo ongazifaka ngezindlela eziningi ezahlukene. 
- Ungaqala ukufaka isandla namhlanje ngokufaka umkhiqizo kusuka ebhodini lakho lokudla. 

*** Thola amaqiniso ***
- I-Open Food Facts ikusiza ukuba wenze umqondo wokuphrinta okuhle kumalebula womkhiqizo. Uzokwazi ukuthola (ngokusho izinkomba ezikhona emikhiqizo):
- isikhuthazo se-carbon (ukukhishwa kwe-CO2) nokupakisha (kanye nemiyalelo yokuvuselela),
- I-Nutriscore (amaphuzu okunomsoco), izakhi, amanothi / amanoni, amafutha amaningi agcwele, ama-carbohydrate, ushukela, i-fibre, amaprotheni nosawoti kanye ne-sodium.
- ama-brand, ama-allergen, amalebula (i-bio, i-gluten yamahhala, i-vegan, i-vegetarian, i-halal, i-kosher ...), ulwazi lwe-traceability (amakhodi wokupakisha, umsuka wezithako)
- Ngewayini newayini, uzothola okuqukethwe kotshwala.

*** Isebenza kahle nganoma yisiphi isimo ***
- Ungangeza imikhiqizo emisha, noma ngabe awunawo uxhumano lwe-inthanethi okwamanje.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza luthwala kuphela okudingekayo okunciphisa ukunciphisa idatha kanye nokusetshenziswa kwebhethri
- Awudingi ikhamera ukuze ubuke umkhiqizo. Ungaphinda uthayiphe ibhakhodi uma ungenayo ikhamera noma unenkinga nayo.

*** Ukudla kwakho, idatha yakho ***
- Idatha yakho ingeyakho, futhi ayithunyelwanga ku-intanethi
- Ungasebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza ngokungaziwa
- Ungathumela umlando wakho wokuskena
