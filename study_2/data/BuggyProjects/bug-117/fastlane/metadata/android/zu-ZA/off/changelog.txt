Inguqulo entsha yomdabu ye-Open Food Facts. Wonke amavolontiya e-Open Food Facts ayelokhu esebenza kanzima kuwo, ngokusekelwe kuziphakamiso zakho kanye nempendulo :)
==================
* I-Open Food Facts manje sekunyakaza okuningi kwezwe kunanini ngaphambili. Imikhiqizo engu-500k ikhululiwe, abanikeli ababhalisiwe abangu-10K, amazwe angu-182. Uzojoyina nathi?
==================
Yini entsha ?
==================
1. Uthola isithombe se-360: ukudla okunomsoco, izithako, nawo wonke amaqiniso okudla, okuvezwa kahle nangokucacile.
2. Thola ibanga le- "NutriScore" lokudla komkhiqizo kuwo wonke imikhiqizo emhlabeni. Qhathanisa imikhiqizo kalula. Kulula kunalokho ukulungisa amathebula okudla.
3. Thola ulwazi oluthe xaxa kusuka ku-Wikipedia. Chofoza kwisigaba sokudla, isithasiselo... ukuze ufunde kabanzi futhi ubone imikhiqizo efanayo.
4. Ungasetha izixwayiso ze-allergenens (njalo kabili hlola ilebuli yangempela wena!)
5. Manje uthole umlando wokuskena ongawuthumela futhi ongashiyi ifoni yakho
6. Ungashukumisa ifoni yakho nganoma yisiphi isikhathi kuhlelo lokusebenza ukuqala ukuskena
7. Manje usungeza imikhiqizo emisha ngisho noma ungaxhunyiwe ku-intanethi
8. Manje usungasiza ukuqedela ukudla okuthandayo ngokuqondile kusuka kuhlelo lokusebenza: thatha izithombe, ugcwalise ulwazi, wenze ukukhanya kokudla kwenzeke
9. Konke kuhamba ngokushesha kakhulu. Imikhiqizo ilayisha ngokusheshisa, ngisho nokuxhumeka okungalungile kweselula.
10. Asisenandaba nedatha yakho yomuntu siqu. Awudingi ukungena ngemvume ukuskena nokufaka isandla.
Okungeziwe! Naphezu kwazo zonke izinto ezintsha, uhlelo lokusebenza lunjengokukhanya njengokungaphambili. Isikhala esisele efonini yakho singcwele.
